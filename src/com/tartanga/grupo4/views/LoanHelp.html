Help: Loan Management

What is this screen for?

Here you can view, search, and manage your loans. You can filter them, add new ones, or delete them when needed.

How to search loans

Use the dropdown menu at the top of the screen to choose how you want to search:

  • By Date: Choose a start and end date, then click "Search".
  • By Interest Rate: Type a number (like 3.5) and click "Search".
  • By Amount: Enter the total amount you are looking for and press "Search".

What does the table show?

The table shows the following loan details:

  • Loan ID: Unique number for the loan. (Can't be changed)
  • Start Date: When the loan begins. (You can change it)
  • End Date: When the loan ends. (You can change it)
  • Total Amount: The full amount of the loan. (You can change it)
  • Remaining Amount: What’s left to pay. (Can't be changed)
  • Interest Rate: Percentage applied to the loan. (You can change it)
  • Payment Period: How often you pay (e.g., monthly). (You can change it)

To edit: Double-click on the field you want to change. Press Enter to save.

What can you do?

At the bottom of the screen, you have these buttons:

  • New: Adds a new loan. You can edit its details in the table.
  • Delete: Select a loan in the table, then click "Delete". You’ll be asked to confirm.
